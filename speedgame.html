How to play?

Game objective:

Try to stop the bulb in moving lighting bulb when it is on top of the "goal" bulb, highlighted in red color between blue lines.

Instructions:

Click the "Start" buttton to get light going and press the "Stop" button to try to stop the light on top of the goal bulb. You will be getting one point (+1) for every consecutive time you succed and one minus point (-1) for every failed attempt. Try to reach the highest score in our leaderboard by successfully stopping the bulb as many times in a row as possible. Good luck and have fun!
